Responsible AI Winter School Umeå

11-13 March, 2025

We are pleased to announce the Winter School on Responsible Artificial Intelligence 2025 at Umeå University, Sweden. At this Winter School, we will offer exciting lectures in the field of Responsible Artificial Intelligence as well as helpful hands-on multi-disciplinary and complementary training. The whole event will be accompanied by a poster session and a social program, which will provide opportunities for networking and professional exchange. We look forward to welcoming you to Umeå in March 2025!

Monowar Bhuyan & Virginia Dignum

Arrival to venue

The Responsible ML Winter School will take place in the MIT-Building of Umeå University. This is located close to the Universum Bus stop. A parking lot of the university is located close to the Universum. By public transport, the best way to reach the university from the City centre is to take bus lines 2, 5, 8 or 9 to the bus stop Universum (travel time about 10 minutes). If you like to walk, then it’ll take around 25 minutes.

Submission of poster

Please upload your poster as PDF and a corresponding abstract here by 28th February 2025. This will help us to estimate how many posters will be presented at the poster session.

Important: Please make sure that you bring a printed poster in size A1 for the event itself.

You have creative freedom in designing the layout, but please ensure the use of clear and easily readable fonts and font sizes, as well as legible color contrasts.
